How Does It Work?

Book travel, earn commission — it really is that simple.

Every time travel is booked through your business — flights, hotels, cruises, car hire, excursions and more — you earn anywhere between 5% and 20% commission back in your pocket.

Earn while you travel

You're going to travel anyway — so why not get paid for it? Every trip you book for yourself puts commission back in your pocket instead of someone else's.

Book for others

Friends, family, colleagues, clients — every booking you make on someone else's behalf earns you commission too. Their dream trip, your payday.

Unlimited bookings

There's no cap on how many trips you can book. The more you book, the more you earn — it's that straightforward.

Residual income

Non-compulsory, but sharing this opportunity with others can see you be paid twice as many times per month than just booking travel...
