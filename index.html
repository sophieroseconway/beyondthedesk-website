Welcome to Beyond The Desk

CEO: Sophie Conway

I'm Sophie, a physiotherapist by trade, but beyond that, someone who has always craved more freedom, experiences, and fulfillment.

After completing two degrees and building a career across professional sport and in the NHS, I realised that even with those achievements, something was missing. The traditional pathway didn't fully align with the lifestyle I envisioned.

In 2023, alongside my postgraduate studies, I started building an online travel business with no prior experience — initially to monetise the trips I was already booking. Since then, I've travelled more, created an additional income stream, and built something that allows me to work flexibly from anywhere. More importantly, I'm creating a lifestyle that offers greater time freedom, financial flexibility, and choice.

Now, I help others do the same. Whether you're always planning your next trip, looking for additional income, or simply know you want more — there is an opportunity to build something alongside your current life.

If you're ready to create more freedom, this could be your next step.
